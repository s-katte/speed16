3D Printer

What is 3D Printer?

  • A 3D printer is printing device that creates three-dimensional objects. 3D printer receives digital data from a computer as input. However, instead of printing the output on paper, a 3D printer gives a three-dimensional model out of a custom material (filament).
  • Watch this Introductory Video: https://youtu.be/Vx0Z6LplaMU

Application Areas of 3D Printing:

  • Product Research and Development
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Functional Prototyping
  • Concept Modeling
  • Manufacturing Aids
  • End-use Parts
  • Architectural Models
